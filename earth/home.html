What Is Cultural Heritage

  • Art, music, literature, architecture, sculpture, philosophy, religion and science can be seen as aspects of culture. However, culture also includes the customs, traditions, festivals, ways of living and one’ös outlook on various issues of life.
  • Culture varies from place to place and country to country. Its development is based on the historical process operating in a local, regional or national context.
